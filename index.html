Tidbits of my life

  • 2011

    Masters in International Business

    I chose HULT International Business School, and attended their London, Dubai, and Shanghai campuses. Had an extremely diverse student body, and I made friends hailing from all corners of the world. I really enjoyed that class content was tailored to the region we were in.

  • 2012 Masters in Environmental Management

    I went to Instituto de Empresa (IE Business School) in Madrid, Spain. This is by far my favorite educational program. Topics included Ecology, Wetland management, sustainability, renewable energy, and climate science. I am a fan of Spanish food, language, and culture.

  • 2016 Coding Journey

    I started my coding journey in 2016. I am eager to learn as much as I can, and encourage others to pursue programming and other STEM fields.
